Meet Our Founder

Mr. Veerasamy

A visionary educator and philanthropist, Veerasamy established Vallalar Nursery and Primary School in 1991 with a mission to provide quality education rooted in strong values.

With over 20+ years of experience in education, Veerasamy has been instrumental in shaping young minds and creating a nurturing environment that fosters academic excellence and character development.
